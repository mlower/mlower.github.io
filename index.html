About

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Astrophysics and Supercomputing at Swinburne University of Technology and the CSIRO Astronomy and Space Science institute.

My research focusses on studying the properties of pulsars, rotating neutron stars which emit beams of electromagnetic radiation. I observe pulsars using radio telescopes and model their physical and statistical properties using Bayesian inference techniques. These include observations taken by the MeerKAT telescope, an SKA pathfinder located in South Africa, the Parkes radio telescope, and the upgraded Molonglo Observatory Synthesis Telescope (UTMOST).

I am also a member of the LIGO Scientific Collaboration, primarily involved with follow-up parameter estimation of detected of gravitational-wave events.
