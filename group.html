Research themes

  • Pulsar magnetospheres: Understanding the structure and dynamics of pulsar magnetospheres, including plasma processes, particle acceleration, and emission mechanisms.
  • Pulsar spin-down evolution: Examining the long-term changes in pulsar rotation rates and their implications for the short- and long-term evolution of neutron stars.
  • Fast radio bursts: Investigating the origins and properties of fast radio bursts (FRBs), including their potential connection to magnetars and pulsars.
  • Relativistic astrophysics: Studying the effects of strong gravity and relativistic phenomena in neutron star binaries, including precision tests of general relativity.
